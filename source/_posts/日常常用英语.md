## 日常寒暄

### 见面与告别

- You're awake! / You're up! 你醒啦
- There you are! 你来啦
- Still up? 还没睡啊
- Take a raincheck. 改天吧
- Be there or be square. 不见不散

### 吃喝招待

- Help yourself. 随便吃
- Help yourself to more. / Dig in some more! 多吃点
- I'll walk you out. 我送送你吧
- How long are you staying? 你准备待几天啊？
- Give you guys a sneak peek. 给你们先睹为快
- I'm here to see Mr. Johnson. 我来拜访约翰先生
- Let's meet every other week. 每隔一周见一次面吧

### 随口聊

- Just checking. 随口问问
- I just said it off the top of my head. 随口说的
- I just threw it in. 顺手做的
- I just picked it up. 顺便买的
- Force of habit. 习惯了
- What are the odds? / What a coincidence! 好巧啊（odds /ɒdz/ 可能性；几率；coincidence /kəʊˈɪnsɪdəns/ 巧合；一致）
- This is awkward. 好尴尬啊（awkward /ˈɔːkwərd/ 令人尴尬的）
- Are you hitting on me? 你在撩我吗（hit on sb 表示向某人搭讪、与某人调情）

### 对话推进

- Just hear me out. 听我说完
- I'm all ears. 洗耳恭听
- Cut to the chase. 说重点
- Can we drop this? 我们可不可以不要再说这个了？
- Humor me. 你就迁就我一下吧
- I'm lost. 我没听懂/跟不上
- Let me get this straight. 让我捋一捋（get this straight 捋直）
- It doesn't make sense. 这说不通啊（make sense 说得通）
- Between us. 你知我知
- It's a long story. 一言难尽
- That's a deal. 一言为定
- It's a one-time thing. 下不为例
- Just go ahead. / I'll leave you to it. / Go ahead with what you're doing. 你忙你的
- Go with the flow. 顺其自然
- Just my two cents. 个人的一点小看法
- We are on the same page. 我们意见一致
- We are in the same boat. 我们处境相同
- We are playing it by ear. 我们见机行事
- I second that. 我附议/我赞同（这里 second 表示附议）
- I'm down for that. 我同意/我参加（这里 down 是形容词，认同的）

### 出门准备

- Fasten your bag properly. 包包拉好
- Zip up your jacket. 衣服拉上（zip up 是拉上拉链的意思）
- Button up your shirt. 针对衬衫类，button up 意思是扣上纽扣
- Put up the (sun) umbrella. 撑开阳伞吧
- Slap on some sunscreen. 涂点防晒霜（slap on 快速、随意地涂抹某物）
- Grab some snacks out. / Get the snacks out. 拿点零食出来吃
- Don't wander off. / Stay close! 别乱跑（wander off 无意识地走开）
- Stick with the group. / Keep up with us! 跟上队伍（stick with 紧跟某人/群体，不分开）
- bundle up. 多穿点

### 方位与移动

- Come over here. 往这边站点
- Hurry over here! 快来这里
- Go over there. / Head over there. 去那边
- Move inside a bit. 往里面走点
- Hurry up! / Speed up! 走快点 【和come on一样不太礼貌，少用！】
- Look ahead. / Look forward. 往前面看
- Look at the top-right corner. 看右上角那里
- Scoot over a bit. / Move over a little. 坐过去一点

- Speed up! / Step on it! 开快点（开车）
- Pull over. 靠边停车吧

## 词句活用

### 无灵主语

委婉拒绝、询问、请求等场合，用事物/情况作主语往往更礼貌：

- Your response is unacceptable. 我没法接受你的答复
- That's all I can do to help. 我只能帮你这么多了
- What brings you here? 你来这有何贵干？
- What took you so long? 你为什么这么晚？
- What led you to this conclusion? 为什么得出这样的结论
- Your application is not approvable. 我不能批准你的申请
- Your report requires additional information. 你需要在报告上补充信息

物体作主语，句子更简洁：

- The bathroom is occupied. 厕所有人了
- The noise woke me up. 我被噪音吵醒了
- The skirt has two pockets. 那条裙子上有两个口袋
- Coffee wakes me up. 我喝咖啡来提神
- Chocolate cheers me up. 我吃巧克力来让自己开心
- Music relaxes me. 我喜欢听音乐来放松
- The document requires your signature. 你需要在文件上签下名

拟人/客观主语，表达更自然：

- I'll go camping if the weather permits. 我想去露营如果天气好的话
- We'll drive there if the road condition allows. 如果路况好，我们就开车去
- The dinner menu presents a wide choice of dishes. 晚餐菜单上有丰富的食物
- Confidence comes from character, not our looks. 自信源自品质，而不是长相【来自TED】
- New technologies will require new perspectives to break from the way it's always been done. 打造新的技术需要新的思想来打破常规【来自TED】
- New breakthroughs demand new concepts. 要实现新的突破需要全新的理念
